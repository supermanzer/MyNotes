Recipe Ingredient

From Recipe Ingredient
Go to text →

This model is the associative entity that relates ingredients to recipes. It stores data that are unique to this relationship, i.e. quantity and unit.

FieldTypeDescription
IDBigIntPrimary Key
RecipeIDBigIntFK relating to Recipe
IngredientIDBigIntFK relating to Ingredient
QuantityIntQuantity of the ingredient in the recipe
UnitTextThe unit the quantity represents (e.g. cup, pinc, teaspoon)

Steps
